# Brand Style Guide — Вышка Лига

## Brand Identity
Vyshka Liga is a premium infobusiness transformation brand. Positioning: elite business club for experts who want to move to premium segment. Tone: authoritative, luxurious, aspirational.

## Color Palette
| Role | Hex |
|------|-----|
| Background | `#000000` |
| Surface | `#0A0A0A` |
| Elevated | `#1A1A1A` |
| **Gold (primary accent)** | **`#C9A063`** |
| Gold bright | `#D4AF6A` |
| Success / After | `#4A7C59` |
| Problem / Before | `#8B4444` |
| Text | `#FFFFFF` |
| Text muted | `rgba(255,255,255,0.5)` |

Gradients:
- Gold: linear-gradient(135deg, #D4AF6A, #C9A063)
- Warm dark: linear-gradient(160deg, #1A1410 0%, #0A0A0A 100%)

## Typography
- Display/Headlines: **Bebas Neue** — condensed ALL CAPS, monumental
- Body: **Inter Tight** 700-800 weight

## Visual Aesthetic
Dark luxury / cinematic premium. Think: expensive hotel lobbies, warm amber candlelight, gold jewelry on black velvet, executive boardroom.

NOT: techy, neon, startup, bright/pastel, flat design.

## Background Rule
Always dark warm real environments — never flat black voids. Darkened offices, night city, luxury interiors, gold textures.
Lighting: dramatic chiaroscuro — warm gold rim light, deep shadows.

## Thumbnail Rules

### Colors
- Primary: Black + Gold (#C9A063) + White text
- Avoid: blue, cyan, purple, neon

### Typography
- Headlines: bold condensed all-caps (Bebas Neue style)
- Max 1 gold accent word; rest white

### Person
- Right side, shoulders-up, warm dramatic lighting
- Expression: confident authority or impactful surprise
- Attire: premium/business

### Visual Elements (left side)
- Financial data, growth charts, big revenue numbers
- Gold accents, transformation visuals
- Big round numbers: 500K, 3x, 78%

### Text Rule
- Max 3-5 words, massive, white
- Complements title — does NOT repeat it
- Triggers desire loop: pain or transformation
